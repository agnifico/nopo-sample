[ 02 ] The Material

HiPCO® in Your World

Carbon Nanotubes produced by patented HiPCO® (High-Pressure Carbon Monoxide) produces high purity, small-diameter nanotubes of consistent quality to meet the requirement of various applications. The material exhibits a tensile strength 100× that of steel while being ten times lighter; they are 1000× better conductors of electricity than copper and are better heat conductors than diamonds. HiPCO® produced by NoPo is used by customers across various industries.

[ 04 ] Sustainability

Sustainability With Us.

~ 45% As per the Ellen MacArthur Foundation, the production of materials accounts for approximately 45% of global greenhouse gas emissions. A large part of this is on account of high-emission processes like mining, smelting and kiln firing.
▸ ENERGY

The remarkable properties of CNTs — high strength, low weight, high electrical conductivity — make them an excellent choice to replace high-emission materials. Their high surface area and excellent conductivity allow more efficient storage and release of energy in EV batteries.

▸ TRANSPORT

Light weight and high strength make CNTs an excellent choice for reducing heavy copper wires to make lighter aircraft, satellites and vehicles.

▸ WATER

HiPCO nanotubes — mean diameter 0.8 nm — can transport water molecules up to 100× faster than any known pore, resulting in faster and more efficient desalination. Helping alleviate the growing water crisis with less time and energy.
